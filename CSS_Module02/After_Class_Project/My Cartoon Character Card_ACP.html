My Cartoon Character Card


Shin-chan Nohara

About:

  • Age: 5-years-old
  • Favorite Snack: Choco-chips, chocolate
  • Talents: Expert at the "Mr. Elephant" dance and the "butt-alien" walk.
  • He has a pet dog named: Shiro
  • He is honest protagonist
  • Personality: Mischievous, ultra-confident, sarcastic, and completely lacks a filter.
  • Futaba Kindergarten: The school Shin-chan attends, where he drives his teachers crazy with his antics.
  • Action Kamen: Shin-chan's absolute favorite superhero TV show. He constantly imitates the superhero's poses and moves.
  • He often embarrasses his parents with his unfiltered comments
  • Flaws: Hates green peppers, refuses to clean his room, and constantly misinterprets adult idioms.

Family:

  1. Father: Hiroshi Nohara (35 Years Old)

    2. Role: The primary breadwinner working as a low-level manager at Futaba Shoji.

    Traits: Kind-hearted,loves playing golf, and attractive women, but deeply devoted to his family.

    Running Gags: Suffers from notoriously toxic, lethal foot odor that the family uses as a weapon.

  2. Mother: Misae Nohara (29 Years Old)

    3. Role: The fierce, stay-at-home matriarch who keeps the household from collapsing.

    Traits: Short-tempered, loves taking naps, shopping for designer clothes, and gossiping.

    Punishments: Famous for her "drilling" fist-to-temple twist and hitting Shin-chan, creating literal head bumps.

  3. Baby Sister: Himawari Nohara (0-1 Year Old)

    4. Personality: Fast, highly intelligent for a baby, and fiercely stubborn.

    Obsessions: Fascinated by shiny objects, expensive jewelry, and handsome young men.

    Name Origin: Shin-chan chose her name, which means "Sunflower," via a paper airplane game.

  4. Pet Dog: Shiro

    5. Origin: Shin-chan found him abandoned in a cardboard box and begged Misae to keep him.

    Traits: White, fluffy, and looks like a cotton ball.

    Role: The most responsible family member; he often feeds himself and walks himself when Shin-chan forgets.

  5. Grandparents (Extended Family)

    6. Garyu Nohara: Hiroshi’s father from Akita. He is goofy, perverted, and exactly like Shin-chan.

The Kasukabe Defense Organization:

Shin-chan and his friends form the Kasukabe Defense Organization, a group of children who embark on various adventures and protect their town from imaginary threats. The organization includes

  • Kazama:

    • The intelligent and snobbish kid.

  • Nene:

    • A sweet but easily angered girl who loves playing house.

  • Nanako Oohara:

    • A beautiful, kind 20-year-old college student. She is Shin-chan's ultimate crush. Whenever she is around, Shin-chan instantly transforms into a polite, well-behaved gentleman.

  • Masao:

    • The CryBaby of the group.

  • Bo-chan:

    • A stoic, slightly slow, but surprisingly wise boy who loves collecting rocks.

Futaba Kindergarten Staff:

The staff at Shin-chan's school are constantly stressed by his antics but deeply care for the children.

  • Principal Bunta Takakura (The "Boss")

    • The Look: Has a fierce, intimidating face, wears a yellow plaid suit, and looks exactly like a Yakuza mafia leader.

    The Reality: He is a gentle, sweet, and easily frightened man who loves children.

    The Gag: Everyone, especially Shin-chan, accidentally calls him "Kumacho" (Principal Boss/Mafia Boss), which scares the public.

  • Teacher: Midori Yoshinaga (Ishizaka):

    • Role: Shin-chan’s sweet but short-tempered homeroom teacher for the Sunflower Class.

    The Gag: She is locked in a fierce, petty rivalry with Miss Matsuzaka and marries her boyfriend, Matsuzaka Ishizaka, later in the series.

  • Teacher:Ume Matsuzaka:

    • Role: The glamorous, proud teacher of the Rose Class.

    The Flaws: She lies about living a luxury lifestyle but actually lives in a cheap, messy apartment and loves cheap alcohol.

    Tragedy: She is perpetually single and desperate for a handsome husband.

  • Teacher: Masumi Ageo

    • Role: The Tulip Class teacher who joined later in the series.

    The Twist: Extremely shy and timid, but when her glasses are taken off, she becomes aggressive, loud, and brutally honest.
