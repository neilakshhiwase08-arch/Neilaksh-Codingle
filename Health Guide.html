Why your body needs nutrients

Our body needs nutrients to grow, stay healthy, and have energy. Nutrients come from the food we eat and include carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals. Each nutrient has a special job in our body. For example, carbohydrates give us energy, proteins help build muscles, and vitamins keep our immune system strong.
