The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a premier Twenty20 franchise cricket tournament organized by the BCCI, featuring 10 regional teams. These franchises compete in a dynamic double round-robin league and playoff format every year, supported by global player auctions, high-profile sponsorships, and massive international viewership.

The active IPL teams, their home cities, and their championship histories are detailed below:

  • Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB)

    • ~ Owners: Owned by United Spirits Limited (Diageo), with recent consortium backing including the Aditya Birla Group.

    ~ Key Players: Rajat Patidar (Captain), Virat Kohli.

    ~ Titles: 2 (2025, 2026)

  • Mumbai Indians (MI)

    • ~ Owners: Reliance Industries, led by the Ambani Family (Mukesh and Nita Ambani).

    ~ Key Players: Hardik Pandya (Captain), Jasprit Bumrah, Rohit Sharma.

    ~ Titles: 5 (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020)

  • Chennai Super Kings (CSK)

    • ~ Owners: Chennai Super Kings Cricket Limited, closely associated with India Cements and N. Srinivasan.

    ~ Key Players: Ruturaj Gaikwad (Captain), MS Dhoni, Sanju Samson (transferred).

    ~ Titles: 5 (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023)

  • Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR)

    • ~ Owners: Red Chillies Entertainment and the Mehta Group, co-owned by Shah Rukh Khan, Juhi Chawla, and Jay Mehta.

    ~ Key Players: Ajinkya Rahane (Captain), Rinku Singh, Sunil Narine.

    ~ Titles: 3 (2012, 2014, 2024)

  • Gujarat Titans (GT)

    • ~ Owners: Managed and owned by the global private equity firm CVC Capital Partners alongside the Torrent Group.

    ~ Key Players: Shubman Gill (Captain), Kagiso Rabada, Rashid Khan.

    ~ Titles: 1 (2022)

  • Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH)

    • ~ Owners: Sun TV Network, managed prominently by Kalanithi Maran and Kavya Maran.

    ~ Key Players: Pat Cummins (Captain), Travis Head, Heinrich Klaasen.

    ~ Titles: 1 (2016)

  • Rajasthan Royals (RR)

    • ~ Owners: Emerging Media (Manoj Badale), with major investments from an Adar Poonawalla-led consortium and Lachlan Murdoch.

    ~ Key Players: Riyan Parag (Captain), Ravindra Jadeja (transferred), Yashasvi Jaiswal.

    ~ Titles: 1 (2008)

  • Lucknow Super Giants (LSG)

    • ~ Owners: RPSG Group, owned by prominent Indian industrialist Sanjiv Goenka.

    ~ Key Players: Rishabh Pant (Captain), Mohammed Shami, Nicholas Pooran.

    ~ Titles: 0

  • Delhi Capitals (DC)

    • ~ Owners: Jointly owned by corporate powerhouses GMR Group and the JSW Group (Sajjan and Parth Jindal).

    ~ Key Players: Axar Patel (Captain), Kuldeep Yadav, Jake Fraser-McGurk.

    ~ Titles: 0

  • Punjab Kings (PBKS)

    • ~ Owners: Co-owned by Preity Zinta, Ness Wadia, Mohit Burman, and Karan Paul.

    ~ Key Players: Shreyas Iyer (Captain), Arshdeep Singh, Liam Livingstone.

    ~ Titles: 0
