Smart Accessories

Smart Ring

A smart Ring isn like a watch, but it's worn on your finger. It can:

1.Track Heart Rate

2.Count Steps

3.Monitor Sleep

4.Control Phones using Gestures

5.Unlock doors/devices with NFC

6.Make contactless payments

Some Rings work without screens to save battery & look minimal

Cool Factors:-

1.Looks Futuristic

2.Small but Powerful

3.Cyberpunk Vibes

NFC Tags

NFC tags are tiny chips that do actions when tapped with a phone.

Examples:

1.Tap phone= WiFi ON

2.Tap near bed= Alarm set + lights OFF

3.Tap study desk= Opens coding apps

You can stick them:

1.On wall

2.Under Table

3.On keychain

Inside phone cover (Like iPhone cover)

Best Thing: They feel SUPER smart for very low price

AI Translator Earbuds

AI translators earbuds are basically futuristic earbuds that can listen to one language &

translate it into another language almost instantly.

Examples:

1.Tap earbud= Switch language

2.Speak in one language= Hear translation in another language
